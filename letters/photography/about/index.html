Hello, I'm Pius Lee. At the time of writing (03/21/2025), I'm a senior at Westview High School (San Diego, CA) taking Photography 1-2 to fulfill a fine arts requirement. As a part of this requirement, I was tasked to create a collection of photographs of my best works. I take all of my pictures on a mirrorless Panasonic DMC-GF1; it's a very small and light camera based on the Micro Four Thirds system. Since it's so light, I love taking pictures while skiing with this Camera.

In my free time, I work on quantum computing research, machine learning, and physics - you can find my works at plee.app.

For inquiries about my work, you can contact me at cs@plee.app.
